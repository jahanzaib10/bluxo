We are now building the enterprise-level dashboard at `/dashboard` to display financial intelligence and real-time business performance.

This is the most critical visual hub in the app. The dashboard must summarize core financial KPIs and breakdowns for decision-makers.

---

✅ SECTION 1: KPI CARDS (TOP METRICS)

Build 4 responsive cards at the top with:

1. **Total Income**
2. **Total Expenses**
3. **Net Income** (income - expenses - client-linked subscriptions)
4. **Recurring Revenue** (total income where `is_recurring = true`)

Style:
- Icon per card (💰, 📉, 📊, 🔁)
- Up/down % from last month (optional for now)
- Colors (green = income, red = expenses)

---

📈 SECTION 2: INCOME VS EXPENSE TRENDS (CHART)

- Line or area chart comparing monthly `income` vs `expenses`
- X-Axis: months
- Y-Axis: amount
- Tooltip: total per month
- Legend for both lines

---

📉 SECTION 3: EXPENSE BREAKDOWN BY CATEGORY

- Donut or bar chart
- Show top 5 expense categories (rolled up by parent)
- “View All Categories” button → optional redirect

---

💼 SECTION 4: CLIENT REVENUE CONTRIBUTION

- Bar chart or ranked table:
  - Client name + total income + % of total
  - Only for `type = income`

---

📊 SECTION 5: SUBSCRIPTION COSTS

- Small table listing top 5 recurring expenses
- Columns: `name`, `amount`, `billing_cycle`, `next_due`
- Highlight overdue or upcoming renewals

---

🧠 Net Profit Calculation (Critical Logic)

Net Profit =  
`SUM(income.amount)`  
– `SUM(expenses.amount)`  
– `SUM(subscriptions.amount WHERE subscription_type = 'client')`

✅ Scoped by organization  
✅ Filterable by date range (default: current month)

---

🎛 FILTERING CONTROLS

Add filters above charts:
- Date range (month/year or calendar)
- Category
- Client
- Currency (optional)
- Reset button
